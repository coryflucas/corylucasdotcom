About

TLDR: I'm a software engineer and technology leader based in the Atlanta, GA area.
With over two decades of experience in software development, I've mostly worked in the financial services and healthcare industries. While my primary expertise lies in C# and Java, I am passionate about exploring new paradigms and practices, which has led me to experiment with functional programming languages and other more niche technologies. I have worked extensively in public cloud, utlizing infrastructure as code to automate deployments and leveraging systems built on open standards like Kubernetes to create portable architectures.
I am passionate about leadership and mentoring, and I enjoy helping others grow in their careers. Over the course of my career journey, I have provided both direct and indeirect leadership to engineering teams. While I've chosen to pursue high level individual contributor path on my career, I consider thought leadership, mentoring, and educating to be critical parts of my role. Helping grow the next generation of software engineers is something I take great pride in, and I strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive and succeed.
